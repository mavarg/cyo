Getting moving

"All truly great thoughts are concieved by walking."

You know shelter-in-place doesn’t mean you move places? I suppose I’m not here to judge. You decide that people weren’t playing around when they talked about this whole “cabin fever” thing and break the mold by getting out of the house. After strapping up with a set of gloves and a mask, you set out and take a few routes you wouldn’t typically, just to add some spice. It may have been filtered through your face covering, but it’s hard to overstate the impact some fresh air can have.
